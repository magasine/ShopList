Legal · Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · Effective immediately

Short version: Shop List stores your data exclusively on your own device and, optionally, in your personal Google Drive account. We do not collect, sell, or share any personal information. We have no servers of our own.

01

Who we are

Shop List is a personal productivity PWA (Progressive Web App) developed and maintained by @magasine (linktr.ee/magasine). The app runs entirely in your browser and has no backend servers.

02

What data we collect

We collect no personal data of our own. The app stores the following data exclusively on your own devices:

  • Your shopping list items (names, quantities, categories, stock status)
  • App preferences and UI state (sort mode, active filters, theme)
  • Google OAuth access token — stored in your browser's localStorage, never sent to our servers
  • Google Drive folder ID — if you choose to enable Drive sync

✗ not collected  Name, email, location, device identifiers, usage analytics, crash reports, advertising IDs, or any other personal information.

03

Google Drive integration

Shop List offers optional synchronization with Google Drive. This feature is entirely opt-in. If you choose to enable it:

  • The app requests OAuth 2.0 authorization directly from Google using two scopes: drive.file (write access to files the app creates) and drive.readonly (read access to files shared with you)
  • Your shopping list is saved as a JSON file in a folder named _ShopList_Sync in your Google Drive
  • The app communicates directly with Google's servers — no data passes through our infrastructure
  • You can revoke access at any time via myaccount.google.com/permissions or through the app's disconnect button

We do not read, store, or process any content from your Google Drive beyond the specific JSON files created by this app.

04

Data sharing and third parties

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise share your data with any third party. The only external service the app communicates with is Google (for authentication and Drive sync), and that communication goes directly from your browser to Google's servers.

We have no advertising partners, analytics providers, or data brokers.

05

Data retention and deletion

All data is stored locally in your browser. You can delete it at any time by:

  • Using your browser's "Clear site data" or "Clear storage" function
  • Uninstalling the PWA from your device
  • Deleting the _ShopList_Sync folder from your Google Drive (for cloud copies)

We retain no copies of your data on any server.

06

Children's privacy

Shop List does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app does not target children and contains no features designed for minors. If you believe a child has provided personal information via this app, please contact us and we will address it promptly.

07

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
